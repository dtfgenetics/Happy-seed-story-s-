# Risk Register

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This register identifies risks before they become expensive story, science, art, classroom, publishing, or marketing problems.

## Risk Table

| Risk ID | Area | Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Prevention Rule | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| HSS-RISK-001 | Story | Book feels like a worksheet instead of a story. | High | Medium | Story must work emotionally before back matter is added. | Active |
| HSS-RISK-002 | Science | Germination claims become too universal. | High | Medium | Use many, some, often, may, and can where exceptions exist. | Active |
| HSS-RISK-003 | Art | Benny becomes a mature plant too early. | High | Medium | Book 1 ends with Benny as a tiny sprout only. | Active |
| HSS-RISK-004 | Files | Drafts drift across GitHub, Docs, and Drive. | High | High | GitHub source, Docs review copy, Drive asset, and tracker row must cross-reference. | Active |
| HSS-RISK-005 | Classroom | Activity lacks safety language. | High | Medium | Every activity needs safety notes and adult-supervision language. | Active |
| HSS-RISK-006 | Publishing | Final art starts before trim and bleed are locked. | High | Medium | No final art until publishing specs are selected. | Active |

## Rule

Every major risk needs a prevention rule and a status.
