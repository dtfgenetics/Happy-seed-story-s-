# Season 1 Blueprint

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Season Title

From Seed to New Seed

## Purpose

Season 1 teaches the basic plant life cycle through eight connected storybooks.

## Season Learning Path

1. Germination
2. Roots
3. Stems
4. Leaves and photosynthesis
5. Flowers and pollination
6. Fruits and seeds
7. Seed dispersal
8. Compost and soil renewal

## Season Emotional Arc

Growing takes time, help, change, connection, and renewal.

## Book Set

| Book ID | Title | Science Topic | Moral Lesson | Classroom Activity |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| HSS-B01 | Benny Bean Wakes Up | Germination | Patience and hidden progress | Watch a Bean Wake Up |
| HSS-B02 | Penny Pea and the Rooty Secret | Roots | Strong foundations matter | Which Way Do Roots Grow? |
| HSS-B03 | Sammy Stem Stands Tall | Stems and transport | Strength means helping others rise | Colored Water Stem Test |
| HSS-B04 | Lola Leaf Catches the Light | Leaves and photosynthesis | Small daily work matters | Light and Leaf Growth |

| HSS-B06 | Freddy Fruit Holds a Surprise | Fruits and seeds | Protecting small things helps the future | Seed Hunt |
| HSS-B07 | Windy Seed Takes a Trip | Seed dispersal | Change can carry you where you need to grow | Design a Traveling Seed |
| HSS-B08 | Grandma Compost's Hidden Feast | Compost and soil renewal | Nothing useful is truly wasted | Mini Compost Observation Jar |

## Rule

Season 1 should feel like one connected learning journey, not eight unrelated books.
