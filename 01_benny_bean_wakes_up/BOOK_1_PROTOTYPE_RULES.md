## Purpose

Book 1 is the prototype for the entire Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley series. Its structure, back matter, classroom activity pattern, art direction format, and publishing metadata format should become the repeatable standard for future books.

## Locked Book Direction

Title: Benny Bean Wakes Up  
Science Topic: Germination  
Moral Lesson: Patience means trusting growth while it happens in stages.  
Main Problem: Benny thinks nothing is happening because he cannot see himself growing yet.  
Story Type: Underground germination adventure.  
Ending Requirement: Benny breaks through the soil as a tiny sprout, realizes he has started but is not finished, comforts another waiting seed, and sets up Book 2 by leading Penny toward the root mystery.

## Book 1 Science Lock

Approved wording:

- Many seeds begin germination when they have moisture, oxygen, suitable temperature, and time.
- Water helps the seed swell and begin changing.
- The seed coat softens or cracks.
- The first root, called the radicle, often appears before the shoot.
- The shoot grows upward.
- Light becomes important after leaves emerge.

Do not say:

- All seeds need light to germinate.
- Sunlight magically wakes the seed underground.
- Every seed grows the same way.
- Benny becomes a full plant by the end.

## Gate Rule

Books 2–8 should not outrun Book 1. Book 1 must establish the final repeatable system first.
