## Locked Direction

Benny Bean thinks nothing is happening because he cannot see himself growing yet. He learns that growth can begin quietly and in stages. He ends as a tiny sprout, not a full plant.

## Science Lock

Many seeds begin germination when they have moisture, oxygen, suitable temperature, and time. The first root often appears before the shoot. Light becomes important after leaves emerge.
