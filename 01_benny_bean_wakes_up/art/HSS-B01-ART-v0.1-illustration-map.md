## Purpose

This file will hold the page-by-page illustration production map for Book 1.

## Production Warning

Final illustration prompts should not be created until the manuscript text and page purposes are approved.

## Style Anchor

Warm premium early-reader storybook style, colorful Seed Valley world, rounded friendly characters, expressive faces, soft natural lighting, magical but scientifically grounded plant-growth setting.

## Required Fields Per Page

- Page number
- Exact story text
- Scene purpose
- Characters shown
- Character placement
- Expressions
- Background / location
- Science detail
- Emotional beat
- Composition notes
- Text-safe area
- Continuity notes
- What should not appear

## Book 1 Key Visual Sequence

1. Benny as a closed seed in soft soil.
2. Moisture arrives gently.
3. Benny swells slightly.
4. Soil world is revealed.
5. Sol warms above, light later message preserved.
6. Seed coat cracks.
7. First root appears and grows downward.
8. Shoot grows upward.
9. Tiny sprout emerges.
10. Benny comforts another waiting seed.

## Do-Not-Show Rules

- Do not show Benny as a mature plant.
- Do not show shoot before root.
- Do not show sunlight magically forcing germination.
- Do not flood Benny with water.
- Do not overcrowd every page with all characters.
