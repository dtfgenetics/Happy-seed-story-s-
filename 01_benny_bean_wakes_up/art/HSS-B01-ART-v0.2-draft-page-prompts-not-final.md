## Purpose

This file provides draft visual prompts for Book 1. These are not final generation prompts. They are working prompts to test visual direction, character consistency, composition, and science continuity.

## Master Style Block

Warm premium early-reader children’s storybook illustration, charming rounded animated-style characters, expressive faces, soft natural lighting, colorful cozy Seed Valley plant-science world, gentle magical feeling, clear educational storytelling, scientifically grounded germination sequence, soft natural textures, clean text-safe space.

## Global Negative Rules

Do not show Benny as a mature plant. Do not show shoot before root. Do not show sunlight magically forcing germination underground. Do not flood the seed. Do not make soil look like food. Do not overcrowd every page. Do not add random unrelated characters.

## Front Cover Prompt Draft
