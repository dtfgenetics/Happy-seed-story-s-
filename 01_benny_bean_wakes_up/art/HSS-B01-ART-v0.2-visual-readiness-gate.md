## Visual Production Rule

The visual blueprint can be drafted now. Final illustrations should wait until:

1. Manuscript is approved for illustration.
2. Science language is source-verified.
3. Publishing trim size and page layout direction are selected.
4. Character designs are approved.
5. Page-by-page illustration map is complete.

## Book 1 Visual Sequence Lock

1. Benny begins as a closed bean seed underground.
2. Drip arrives and moisture gently dampens the soil.
3. Benny swells slightly.
4. Wally reveals soil spaces and underground life.
5. Sol warms above; light is for later leaves.
6. Benny’s seed coat cracks.
7. First root appears and grows downward.
8. Shoot grows upward.
9. Benny emerges as a tiny sprout.
10. Benny comforts another seed and Penny notices the root mystery.

## Do-Not-Show Rules

- Do not show Benny as a mature plant in Book 1.
- Do not show shoot before root.
- Do not show sunlight magically waking the seed underground.
- Do not flood Benny with water.
- Do not make soil look like food.
- Do not overcrowd every page with the whole cast.
- Do not add random background characters that distract from the science stage.
