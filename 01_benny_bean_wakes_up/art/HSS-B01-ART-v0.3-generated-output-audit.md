## Audit Verdict

The recent generated cover, Page 1, audit boards, and contact-sheet outputs are rejected. They do not reliably match the user-approved master visual reference and must not be treated as production art.

## Confirmed Failures

1. Character drift
- Benny was repeatedly rendered as a tan potato-like or generic oval character instead of the exact approved Benny design.
- Eye spacing, body silhouette, facial proportions, limb placement, and surface treatment changed between outputs.
- Penny and the supporting cast were improvised rather than held to approved model sheets.

2. Wrong rendering style
- Several outputs used glossy 3D, CGI, or Pixar-like rendering.
- The approved direction is soft 2D children’s storybook illustration with warm painterly texture, rounded shapes, gentle natural lighting, and a handmade educational-book feeling.

3. Wrong production format
- The generator repeatedly created audit posters, contact sheets, and multi-page boards instead of one finished landscape book page.
- Book production requires one 8.5 × 11 landscape page per approved output.

4. Uncontrolled text
- Story wording, titles, page numbers, labels, and captions were generated inside the artwork.
- This caused altered wording, incorrect page sequence, spelling risk, and layout drift.
- Final illustration files must contain no generated story text. Exact manuscript text must be placed separately during layout.

5. Page sequence and science drift
- Some contact sheets changed the 32-page structure and moved germination milestones.
- Locked sequence remains: no real root before Page 18; first radicle on Page 18; no shoot before Page 20; no above-ground sprout before Page 23; Benny remains a tiny sprout at the end of Book 1.

6. Master-reference conflict
- Existing text-only planning files contain provisional descriptions that conflict with the user’s approved visual boards.
- When text notes and approved visual references conflict, the user-approved visual reference wins.

## Production Decision

FINAL PAGE GENERATION IS PAUSED.

No new cover or story page should be approved until all of the following are complete:

- the original approved roadmap manifesto / character reference images are stored in the Book 1 Character Sheets folder and in the GitHub asset-reference directory;
- Benny, Penny, Drip, Wally, and Sol each have a locked model sheet;
- Benny’s Book 1 seed-stage model is separated from his series mascot model;
- the page illustration is generated without text;
- exact text is added in a controlled layout pass;
- each output passes character, style, science, crop, and wording QA.

## Locked Art Direction

- 8.5 × 11 landscape.
- One book page per output.
- Soft 2D painterly children’s storybook style.
- Warm natural palette; cream text-safe regions; dark green/brown layout typography added later.
- No glossy 3D, plastic, photorealistic, hyper-detailed, or Pixar-like rendering.
- No contact sheets or audit posters as book pages.
- No AI-generated story text inside illustration art.
- Clear text-safe space planned into each composition.
- Same approved character proportions, colors, face, and silhouette on every page.

## Page 1 Rebuild Requirements

Page 1 must be rebuilt only after the model sheet is locked.

Scene:
- wide Seed Valley cutaway at dawn;
- gentle hills, flowers, and distant garden homes above ground;
- cozy, non-scary soil chamber below;
- Benny resting as a completely closed seed;
- no crack, root, shoot, sprout, leaf, water character, or additional cast;
- open text-safe area in the upper-left sky/soil transition;
- illustration-only output with no words or page number.

## QA Rule

A visually attractive image that does not match the master character design is a failed image. It must be recreated rather than patched into the book.
