## Purpose

This file defines the Book 1 character design and continuity notes before illustration prompts are created.

## Benny Bean

Role: main character.  
Science function: seed germination.  
Emotional function: patience and hidden progress.  
Personality: impatient, funny, nervous, dramatic, eager.  
Visual direction: friendly bean seed character, rounded, expressive face, small body language.  
Continuity: starts as a closed seed, swells, cracks, first root appears, shoot emerges, ends as a tiny sprout.

## Penny Pea

Role: curious observer and friend.  
Science function: noticing, asking, recording clues.  
Emotional function: calm support.  
Personality: thoughtful, gentle, observant.  
Visual direction: small pea character with curious expression and simple notebook or observation cue.  
Continuity: supports Benny but does not become the Book 1 hero.

## Drip Water Drop

Role: moisture helper.  
Science function: water helps seed swell and begin changing.  
Emotional function: cheerful encouragement.  
Personality: playful, bouncy, optimistic.  
Visual direction: round water drop with bright expressive face.  
Continuity: shows moisture gently, not flooding.

## Wally Worm

Role: soil helper.  
Science function: soil spaces, tunnels, underground life.  
Emotional function: practical humor.  
Personality: friendly, earthy, useful, funny.  
Visual direction: soft rounded worm character with warm expression.  
Continuity: do not imply worms alone create all soil air spaces.

## Sol the Sunbeam

Role: warmth and light helper.  
Science function: warmth now, light later.  
Emotional function: warm reassurance.  
Personality: gentle, patient, wise.  
Visual direction: soft golden sunbeam with kind expression.  
Continuity: does not magically force germination underground.

## Do-Not-Change Rules

- Benny cannot become a mature plant in Book 1.
- The root appears before the shoot.
- Sol cannot be shown as the sole cause of germination.
- Drip cannot flood the seed.
- Penny cannot replace Benny as the emotional lead.
