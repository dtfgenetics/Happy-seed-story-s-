## Purpose

This file will convert the locked manuscript into page-by-page illustration directions.

## Rule

Do not build final page prompts until the manuscript is approved.

## Required Page Prompt Fields

For every page include:

- Page number
- Exact text on page
- Scene purpose
- Characters shown
- Character placement
- Facial expressions
- Background / location
- Science detail
- Emotional beat
- Composition notes
- Lighting notes
- Text-safe area
- Continuity notes
- What should NOT appear

## Book 1 Continuity Lock

- Benny begins as a closed bean seed.
- Benny may swell slightly after moisture.
- Seed coat cracks before radicle appears.
- Radicle appears first and grows downward.
- Shoot grows upward after root appears.
- Benny ends as a tiny sprout, not a mature plant.
- Sol may warm the scene but must not magically force germination.
