## Words That Grew

### Seed

A tiny plant beginning that can grow when conditions are right.

### Germination

When a seed begins to grow.

### Seed Coat

The outer covering that helps protect a seed.

### Moisture

A small amount of water.

### Oxygen

A gas in air that living things need. Seeds use oxygen during germination.

### Suitable Temperature

A temperature that is right for a seed to begin growing.

### Radicle

The first tiny root that often grows from a seed.

### Root

The plant part that usually grows downward, helps hold the plant, and absorbs water and dissolved minerals.

### Shoot

The young plant part that grows upward.

### Sprout

A young plant that has just begun to grow above the surface.

### Observe

To look carefully and notice changes.

## Vocabulary Rule

Use these words in the story only when they feel natural. Define them clearly in back matter.
