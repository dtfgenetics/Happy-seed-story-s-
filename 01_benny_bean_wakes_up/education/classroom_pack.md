## Activity

Title: Watch a Bean Wake Up  
Question: What changes can we observe as a bean seed germinates?  
Materials: bean seed, clear cup or bag, paper towel, water, observation journal, pencil or crayons.  

## Observation Targets

- seed swelling
- seed coat cracking
- first root
- shoot
- direction of growth
- first tiny leaves

## Baseline Safety Notes

Adult supervision recommended.  
Do not eat experiment seeds.  
Wash hands after handling seeds, soil, or plant materials.  
Keep the paper towel damp, not soaking.  
Do not touch mold if it appears.
