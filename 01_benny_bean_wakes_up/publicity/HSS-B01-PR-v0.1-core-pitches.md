## One-Sentence Pitch

Benny Bean Wakes Up is a warm plant-science story about an impatient seed who learns that important growth can begin before anyone sees it.

## Parent Pitch

Benny Bean Wakes Up helps children understand patience, hidden progress, and the first stages of seed germination through a gentle Seed Valley adventure.

## Teacher Pitch

A classroom-ready read-aloud that introduces germination, observation, sequence, and plant-growth vocabulary through story, back matter, and a simple bean observation activity.

## Library Pitch

A charming early science storybook with strong read-aloud appeal, lovable characters, and educational back matter for curious young readers.

## Homeschool Pitch

A story-based plant science lesson with vocabulary, a germination activity, observation journal prompts, and parent/teacher guidance.

## Brand Position

Story-first plant science with emotional growth and classroom usefulness.
