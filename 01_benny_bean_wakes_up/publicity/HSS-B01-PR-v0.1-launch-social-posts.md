## Post 1 - Parent Audience

Some growth happens before we can see it.

Benny Bean Wakes Up is a warm children’s story about an impatient little seed learning patience, hidden progress, and the first steps of germination.

## Post 2 - Teacher Audience

Looking for a gentle plant-science read-aloud?

Benny Bean Wakes Up introduces germination through story, vocabulary, observation, and a simple bean activity children can follow over time.

## Post 3 - Seed Valley Introduction

Welcome to Seed Valley, where tiny seeds, curious friends, and real plant science grow into magical adventures.

Book 1 begins with Benny Bean, a little seed asking one big question:

Is it time?

## Post 4 - Classroom Activity

Pair the story with a simple observation activity: Watch a Bean Wake Up.

Children can draw what they notice each day as a seed swells, cracks, roots, and sprouts.
