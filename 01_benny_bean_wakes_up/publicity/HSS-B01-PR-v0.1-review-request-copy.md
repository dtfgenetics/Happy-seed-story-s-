## Purpose

This file stores draft outreach copy for future early reviewers, teachers, librarians, parents, and homeschool readers.

## Short Review Request Draft

Hello,

I’m preparing Benny Bean Wakes Up, the first book in the Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley series. It is a warm children’s plant-science story about germination, patience, and hidden growth.

I would be grateful for your feedback on the story, read-aloud flow, educational usefulness, and whether the science feels clear for young children.

Thank you for helping this Seed Valley story grow.

## Teacher Feedback Request

Hello,

I’m developing Benny Bean Wakes Up as a story-based plant science read-aloud with vocabulary, a simple germination activity, and an observation journal prompt.

I would appreciate feedback on whether the book feels useful for a classroom, homeschool, or nature-learning setting.

## Feedback Questions

1. Did the story hold your attention?
2. Was Benny’s emotional journey clear?
3. Was the germination science understandable?
4. Did anything feel too advanced or too simple?
5. Would the activity be usable with children?
6. What should be clearer before publication?
