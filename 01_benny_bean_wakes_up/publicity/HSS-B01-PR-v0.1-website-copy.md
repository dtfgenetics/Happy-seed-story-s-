## Short Product Copy

Benny Bean wants to grow right now, but underground, nothing seems to be happening. With help from his Seed Valley friends, Benny learns that growth can begin quietly before anyone sees it.

## Longer Product Copy

In Benny Bean Wakes Up, children follow an impatient little seed as he waits in the soft soil and wonders when it will finally be his turn to grow. With help from Penny Pea, Drip Water Drop, Wally Worm, and Sol the Sunbeam, Benny discovers that many seeds begin changing in hidden stages before a sprout appears.

This warm story introduces germination, observation, patience, and the idea that small beginnings matter.
