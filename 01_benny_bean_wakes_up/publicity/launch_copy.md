## One-Sentence Pitch

Benny Bean Wakes Up is a warm plant-science adventure about an impatient little seed who learns that real growth can begin before anyone sees it.

## Parent Pitch

A gentle story that helps children understand patience, hidden progress, and the first steps of plant growth.

## Teacher Pitch

A classroom-ready germination story with vocabulary, observation prompts, and a simple bean-sprouting activity.

## Library Pitch

A warm early-reader science story with read-aloud charm, emotional growth, and educational back matter.
