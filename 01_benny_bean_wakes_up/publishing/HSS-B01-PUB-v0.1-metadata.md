## Short Description Draft

Benny Bean wants to grow right now, but nothing seems to be happening. With help from his Seed Valley friends, Benny discovers that important growth can begin before anyone sees it.

## Long Description Draft

Benny Bean is tired of waiting. While the world above the soil seems busy and bright, Benny still feels like a plain little seed. With help from Penny Pea, Drip Water Drop, Wally Worm, and Sol the Sunbeam, Benny learns that many seeds begin growing in hidden stages. Moisture, air, warmth, and time help him change until his seed coat cracks, his first root appears, and he finally becomes a tiny sprout.
