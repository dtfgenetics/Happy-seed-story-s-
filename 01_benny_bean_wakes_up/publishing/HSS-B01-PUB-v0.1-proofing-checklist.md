## Purpose

This checklist will be used after layout exports are created.

## Story Proof Checks

- All page text matches the locked manuscript.
- Page order is correct.
- Page turns still work.
- No text is missing.
- No dialogue is assigned to the wrong character.

## Science Proof Checks

- Germination sequence is correct.
- Root appears before shoot.
- Benny ends as a tiny sprout.
- No page says all seeds need light to germinate.
- Back matter matches SCIENCE_RULES.md.

## Art Proof Checks

- Characters are consistent.
- Text is readable.
- Text is not too close to edges.
- No important science detail is hidden.
- Pages are not overcrowded.

## Classroom Proof Checks

- Activity steps are clear.
- Safety notes are visible.
- Journal page has enough writing/drawing space.
- Vocabulary definitions are readable.

## Publishing Proof Checks

- Trim, bleed, margin, and export settings match selected platform requirements.
- Cover and interior files are named correctly.
- Proof files are stored in Drive.
- Tracker status is updated.
