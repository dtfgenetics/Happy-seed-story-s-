## Source 1 - KDP Trim, Bleed, and Margins

URL: https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/help/topic/GVBQ3CMEQW3W2VL6
Source type: official KDP help page.

Key finding:
KDP states that trim size, bleed, and margins must be set correctly so the book prints correctly. KDP warns that improper sizing can lead to upload rejection or print-quality issues.

Project rule created:
Do not create final illustrations, final interior layout, or final cover files until trim size, bleed, margins, and page count are chosen.

## Source 2 - Bowker ISBN US

URL: https://www.bowker.com/isbn-us
Source type: Bowker official ISBN page.

Key finding:
Bowker is the official ISBN source for the United States ISBN workflow.

Project rule created:
Do not finalize metadata or ISBN fields until the author/publisher/imprint and ownership strategy are decided.

## Source 3 - Library of Congress PrePub Book Link

URL: https://www.loc.gov/programs/prepub-book-link/about-this-program/
Source type: Library of Congress official page.

Key finding:
The Library of Congress PrePub Book Link is the route for eligible prepublication book information workflows.

Project rule created:
Do not pursue cataloging/prepublication steps until title, author, publisher/imprint, format, and ISBN information are more stable.
