## Purpose

This file defines the visual direction for Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley.

## Approved Style Direction

The books should feel like a premium early-reader children’s storybook series with:

- warm colorful animated-style characters
- soft natural lighting
- rounded friendly designs
- expressive faces
- clear educational structure
- magical plant-science world
- charming natural textures
- gentle humor

## Visual Rules

- Characters must stay consistent across pages and books.
- The manuscript controls the image plan.
- Science stages must be visually accurate.
- Do not overcrowd every scene with the full cast.
- Keep text-safe areas clean.
- Magic may enhance the feeling, but cannot replace science.

## Book 1 Growth Continuity

- Benny starts as a closed bean seed.
- Benny may swell slightly after moisture.
- Seed coat cracks before the radicle appears.
- Radicle appears first and grows downward.
- Shoot grows upward after the root appears.
- Benny ends as a tiny sprout, not a mature plant.
- Light helps after leaves emerge.

## Prompt Rule

Every page prompt must include:

- Book ID
- Page number
- Scene purpose
- Characters shown
- Character expressions
- Background/location
- Science detail
- Emotional beat
- Composition notes
- Text-safe area notes
- Do-not-show notes
