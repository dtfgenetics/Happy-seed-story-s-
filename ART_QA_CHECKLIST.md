# Art QA Checklist

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This checklist reviews illustration plans and image outputs before they are approved or locked.

## Style Checks

- Does the art match the approved Seed Valley style?
- Are characters warm, rounded, friendly, and expressive?
- Is the lighting soft and natural?
- Does the page feel premium and storybook-ready?
- Does the scene feel magical without replacing science?

## Composition Checks

- Is the page easy to understand?
- Is the main character or action clear?
- Is the text-safe area clean?
- Is the page not overcrowded?
- Does the image support the page-turn purpose?

## Continuity Checks

- Do characters match their character sheets?
- Do growth stages match the science sequence?
- Does the background location match the page plan?
- Are recurring props, colors, and design cues consistent?

## Book 1 Art Checks

- Benny begins as a seed.
- Seed coat cracks before the first root appears.
- First root grows downward.
- Shoot grows upward.
- Benny ends as a tiny sprout.
- Sol warms but does not magically cause germination.
- Drip shows moisture without flooding the scene.

## Rule

Do not approve art that is beautiful but contradicts the story or science.
