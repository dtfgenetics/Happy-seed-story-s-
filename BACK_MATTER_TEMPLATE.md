## Purpose

This file defines the repeatable educational back matter for each book.

## Required Back Matter Sections

### What We Learned

Kid-friendly explanation of the book's science topic.

### Words That Grew

Vocabulary list with simple definitions.

### Try It Yourself

Safe activity or experiment connected directly to the story.

### Observation Journal

A drawing, writing, measuring, or comparison page.

### Parent / Teacher Guide

Discussion questions, science clarification, safety notes, and extension ideas.

### Next Adventure Teaser

One question or scene that naturally leads to the next book.

## Rule

Back matter should support the story and classroom use, not replace the story.
