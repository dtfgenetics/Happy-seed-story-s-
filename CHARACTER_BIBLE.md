## Purpose

This file defines the recurring cast so character roles, voices, emotional arcs, and visual identities stay consistent across the series.

## Character Rules

- Each book has one emotional lead.
- Supporting characters help but do not steal the story.
- Each recurring character has a science function and an emotional function.
- Visual design must stay consistent across books.
- Character growth should be believable and staged.

## Core Book 1 Cast

### Benny Bean

Role: Book 1 main character.  
Science lane: Germination.  
Emotional lane: Patience and hidden progress.  
Personality: impatient, funny, nervous, dramatic, eager to grow.  
Continuity rule: ends Book 1 as a tiny sprout, not a mature plant.

### Penny Pea

Role: observer and science friend.  
Science lane: observation, questions, recording changes.  
Emotional lane: calm curiosity.  
Personality: thoughtful, gentle, precise, quietly funny.  
Continuity rule: helps Benny but does not take over Book 1.

### Drip Water Drop

Role: water helper.  
Science lane: moisture, water movement, seed swelling.  
Emotional lane: cheerful encouragement.  
Personality: playful, bouncy, optimistic.  
Continuity rule: moisture should be gentle, not flooding.

### Wally Worm

Role: soil helper.  
Science lane: soil spaces, tunnels, root room, underground life.  
Emotional lane: practical humor.  
Personality: friendly, earthy, useful, funny.  
Continuity rule: do not imply worms alone create all useful soil spaces.

### Sol the Sunbeam

Role: warmth and light helper.  
Science lane: warmth now, light later.  
Emotional lane: warm wisdom.  
Personality: gentle, patient, encouraging.  
Continuity rule: Sol does not magically force germination underground.
