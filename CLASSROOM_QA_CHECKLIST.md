# Classroom QA Checklist

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This checklist reviews classroom and home-learning materials before approval.

## Activity Checks

- Is the activity directly connected to the book's science topic?
- Is the science question clear?
- Are the materials low-cost and easy to find?
- Are the steps simple enough for adult-led use?
- Can children observe, draw, count, measure, compare, or describe something?
- Does the activity avoid promising exact results?

## Safety Checks

- Are adult supervision notes included?
- Are handwashing notes included?
- Are choking, allergy, cutting, mold, soil, compost, or outdoor plant cautions included where needed?
- Are unsafe materials avoided?

## Teacher / Parent Checks

- Can a teacher use this without extra explanation?
- Can a parent use this at home?
- Are discussion questions included?
- Is there an extension idea for older children?
- Is the observation journal clear?

## Rule

If the activity is cute but not safe, clear, or repeatable, it is not ready.
