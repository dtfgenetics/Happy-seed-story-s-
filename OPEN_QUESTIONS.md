# Open Questions

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1

Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This file tracks decisions that still need answers before final production, publishing, or launch.

## Publishing Questions

- What is the official author name?
- What is the official illustrator credit plan?
- What publisher or imprint name will be used?
- What trim size will Book 1 use?
- Will Book 1 be paperback, hardcover, ebook, PDF, or multiple formats?
- What platform comes first: KDP, IngramSpark, direct PDF, or another channel?
- What is the ISBN plan?

## Creative Questions

- Is the official series title Happy Seed Stories, Seed Valley, or Happy Seed Stories: Seed Valley?
- Which character designs are final?
- What is the final cover system for the series?
- How many recurring characters should appear in Book 1 art?

## Education Questions

- What grade range should be listed in metadata?
- Should activities be marked by age level?
- Should teacher pages include standards alignment later?
- Will classroom PDFs be free, paid, or bundled with books?

## Production Questions

- Which layout tool will be used for final book design?
- Where will final illustration files be stored in Drive?
- What naming format will final exports use?

## Rule

If the answer is unknown, track it here instead of guessing.
