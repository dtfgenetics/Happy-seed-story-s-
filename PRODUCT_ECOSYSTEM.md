## Purpose

This file defines how Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley can grow beyond single books while staying connected to the core educational story system.

## Core Product Ladder

1. Free printable activity page
2. Single storybook
3. Storybook plus activity PDF
4. Season 1 book bundle
5. Observation journal
6. Vocabulary cards
7. Classroom experiment pack
8. Plant life cycle poster set
9. Teacher guide
10. Garden starter kit
11. Read-aloud video scripts
12. Animated shorts

## Rule

Every product extension must connect back to a book, character, science topic, or classroom activity. Do not create random merchandise that does not support the series mission.

## Book-Based Expansion Template

For every book, create:

- storybook
- classroom activity sheet
- observation journal page
- vocabulary cards
- coloring page
- science poster
- teacher discussion guide
- parent guide
- social media learning post
- website product copy

## Season 1 Bundle Concept

Seed Valley: From Seed to New Seed

Includes:

- Books 1–8
- plant life cycle poster set
- experiment pack
- observation journal
- vocabulary cards
- teacher guide

## Brand Rule

The brand is not just cute plant characters. It is story-first plant science with classroom usefulness and emotional growth.
