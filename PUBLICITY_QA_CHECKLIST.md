# Publicity QA Checklist

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This checklist reviews buyer-facing and launch-facing materials before they are approved.

## Message Checks

- Is the one-sentence pitch clear?
- Does the parent pitch explain emotional and learning value?
- Does the teacher pitch explain classroom usefulness?
- Does the library pitch explain read-aloud and educational value?
- Does the homeschool pitch explain how the book fits a lesson?

## Copy Checks

- Back cover copy is warm and clear.
- Website copy explains the story and science topic.
- Retail descriptions are not too long or too vague.
- Social posts match the brand tone.
- Review request copy is polite and specific.

## Product Checks

- Is there a classroom freebie or activity page?
- Does the book connect to the Season 1 product ladder?
- Does the next book teaser support series reading?

## Rule

Do not market the book only as cute. The message must include story value, science value, and classroom or parent usefulness.
