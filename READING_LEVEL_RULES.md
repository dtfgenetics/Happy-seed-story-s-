Version: v0.1

Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This file defines the target reading and read-aloud level for Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley.

## Audience Direction

Primary reader/listener: ages 5 to 8.  
Secondary audience: ages 4 to 10 with adult support.  
Primary use: adult read-aloud with early-reader-friendly language.

## Story Page Rules

- Keep sentences clear and musical for read-aloud use.
- Use short and medium sentences, not only short choppy lines.
- Avoid long textbook explanations in story pages.
- Use repeated phrases only when they strengthen rhythm or character.
- Let dialogue carry humor and emotion.

## Back Matter Rules

Back matter can use more explanation than story pages, but should remain kid-friendly.

## Vocabulary Rule

Science words should appear naturally in the story and be explained simply later.

## Quality Test

Read the page aloud. If the rhythm feels stiff or the science interrupts the emotion, revise it.
