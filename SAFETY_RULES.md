# Safety Rules

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This file defines baseline safety rules for classroom, homeschool, and parent-led plant activities.

## Baseline Safety Language

Adult supervision recommended.  
Do not eat experiment seeds.  
Wash hands after handling seeds, soil, compost, or plant materials.  
Do not touch unknown outdoor plants.  
Use adult help for cutting fruit, stems, or plant samples.  
Avoid mold exposure. Do not touch mold directly.  
Check allergies before using food or plant materials.  
Keep small seeds and materials away from very young children who may put them in their mouths.

## Required Safety Checks Per Activity

Every activity must answer:

- Are any materials edible or allergen-related?
- Are any small parts a choking risk?
- Is cutting or scissors involved?
- Is soil, compost, mold, or outdoor plant material involved?
- Does the activity require handwashing?
- Does the activity require adult help?

## Compost Activity Rule

Compost activities must avoid meat, dairy, oily foods, pet waste, and unsafe scraps. If mold appears, children should not touch it directly.

## Outdoor Observation Rule

Children should not touch, taste, or collect unknown outdoor plants without adult guidance.
