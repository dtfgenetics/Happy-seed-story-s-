# Science QA Checklist

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This checklist is used to review plant science before a manuscript, illustration map, activity, or back matter file is marked Approved or Locked.

## General Science Checks

- Does the book focus on one main science topic?
- Are all science claims accurate for young readers?
- Are exceptions handled with careful words such as many, some, often, may, or can?
- Are there any false universal claims?
- Does the story avoid making magic replace science?
- Does the activity honestly describe what children may observe?
- Are safety notes included where needed?

## Visual Science Checks

- Does the illustration show the correct plant stage?
- Does the visual sequence match the science sequence?
- Could a child misunderstand the science from the image?
- Are roots, shoots, leaves, flowers, fruits, or soil helpers shown in a misleading way?

## Book 1 Germination Checks

- Does Benny begin as a seed?
- Does moisture arrive before swelling/change?
- Does the seed coat crack before the root appears?
- Does the first root appear before the shoot?
- Does the root grow downward?
- Does the shoot grow upward?
- Is light explained as important after leaves emerge?
- Does Benny end as a tiny sprout, not a mature plant?

## Rule

If the science is wrong, the cute version is not acceptable. Fix the science and keep the charm.
