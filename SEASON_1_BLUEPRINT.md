## Purpose

This file defines the Season 1 learning path and book sequence.

## Season 1 Theme

From Seed to New Seed.

## Season 1 Learning Path

1. Germination
2. Roots
3. Stems
4. Leaves
5. Flowers
6. Fruits
7. Seed travel
8. Soil renewal

## Season 1 Emotional Arc

Growing takes time, help, change, connection, and renewal.
