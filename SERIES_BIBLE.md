## Series Identity

Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley is a premium early-reader children’s plant-science storybook series. Each book teaches real plant science through adventure, friendship, humor, emotional growth, and classroom-ready activities.

## Audience

Primary: ages 5–8.  
Secondary: ages 4–10 with adult support.  
Buyers/users: parents, teachers, homeschool families, libraries, nature programs, garden clubs.

## Core Promise

A child gets:

- a lovable character
- a real story
- a simple science idea
- an emotional lesson
- a safe activity to try
- a reason to read the next book

## Tone

Warm, curious, funny, heartfelt, educational, adventurous.

## Avoid

- worksheet-style storytelling
- forced rhymes
- preachy morals
- false universal science claims
- random art scenes not tied to the manuscript
- too many characters in one book

## Core Formula

1. A real character problem
2. An adventure in Seed Valley
3. A science discovery
4. A solution
5. An earned life lesson
6. Classroom back matter
7. A teaser into the next book

## Season 1 Learning Path

1. Germination
2. Roots
3. Stems
4. Leaves and photosynthesis
5. Flowers and pollination
6. Fruit and seeds
7. Seed dispersal
8. Compost and soil renewal

## Series Rule

The manuscript comes before images. The approved manuscript controls every illustration plan.
