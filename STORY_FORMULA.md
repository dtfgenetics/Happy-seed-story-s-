## Purpose

This file defines the repeatable story engine for every Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley book.

## Core Formula

Every book must include:

1. A real character problem
2. A Seed Valley adventure
3. A real plant-science discovery
4. A practical solution
5. An earned emotional lesson
6. A classroom/home activity
7. A next-book teaser

## Required Story Questions

Before drafting a book, answer:

- Who is the emotional lead?
- What does the character want?
- What is the character misunderstanding?
- What plant-science process reveals the truth?
- What changes emotionally by the end?
- What does the child reader learn?
- What can a parent or teacher do with it?

## Page-Turn Rule

Every book needs clear page-turn moments. Page turns should create curiosity, surprise, emotion, humor, or discovery.

## Moral Rule

The moral must be earned through the character's journey. Do not state the moral like a classroom poster unless the story has already made the child feel it.

## Science Rule

The science should appear through action, observation, dialogue, and discovery, not through long lectures.
