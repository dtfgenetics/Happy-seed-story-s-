## Purpose

This file defines how science vocabulary is introduced, used, and explained in Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley.

## Vocabulary Rules

- Introduce science words in story context before defining them.
- Do not overload one page with too many new words.
- Use simple definitions in back matter.
- Repeat important words naturally across the book.
- Avoid making the story sound like a glossary.

## Word Categories

### Story Words
Words children can understand from context.

### Science Words
Words that need simple definitions.

### Teacher Words
More precise words that can appear in parent/teacher notes.

## Book 1 Core Vocabulary

- seed
- germination
- seed coat
- moisture
- oxygen
- suitable temperature
- radicle
- root
- shoot
- sprout
- observe

## Definition Style

Use simple, accurate definitions.

Example:

Germination: when a seed begins to grow.

Radicle: the first tiny root that often grows from a seed.

## Rule

Vocabulary should help children feel smarter, not slow the story down.
