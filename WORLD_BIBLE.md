## Purpose

This file defines the world of Seed Valley so locations, scale, tone, and science realism stay consistent.

## World Identity

Seed Valley is a warm magical plant-science world where seeds, roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruit, soil helpers, sunbeams, water drops, and garden creatures can become story characters.

## World Tone

- magical but grounded
- cozy and colorful
- friendly and expressive
- adventurous but safe
- science-real under the magic

## Scale Rule

Seed Valley is shown from a small garden-world point of view. Soil crumbs, roots, leaves, drops of water, and tiny tunnels can feel huge to the characters.

## Magic Rule

Magic can make the world feel charming, but it cannot replace science. A sunbeam may glow warmly, but it should not magically force germination. A water drop may talk, but moisture still behaves like water.

## Human Rule

Humans should generally stay off-page or implied unless a specific book or classroom scene requires them. The focus stays inside Seed Valley.

## Continuity Rule

Book locations must match the science topic. Underground science belongs underground. Leaf and light science belongs aboveground. Compost science belongs in soil and decomposer spaces.
