# Character Continuity Rules

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This file prevents character drift across pages, books, illustrations, and promotional assets.

## Global Rules

- Character design must stay consistent across every page.
- Personality must stay consistent with the Character Bible.
- Each character should have a clear emotional function and science function.
- Characters should not change form unless the story and science require it.
- Every transformation must happen in the correct story order.

## Book 1 Benny Continuity

Benny begins as a closed bean seed.  
Benny may swell slightly after moisture.  
Benny's seed coat cracks before the radicle appears.  
The radicle appears first and grows downward.  
The shoot grows upward after the root appears.  
Benny ends as a tiny sprout.  
Benny should not become a full plant in Book 1.

## Supporting Cast Continuity

Penny Pea remains the observer/science friend.  
Drip Water Drop remains playful and water-focused.  
Wally Worm remains soil-focused and practical.  
Sol the Sunbeam remains warm and gentle, not a magic solution.

## Art Review Rule

If a generated image changes a character's body, role, science stage, or personality, it must be rejected or revised.
