# Safety Rules

Project ID: HSS-000  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Drafting  
Source of Truth: GitHub  

## Purpose

This file defines baseline safety language for all Happy Seed Stories classroom and home activities.

## Baseline Safety Notes

Use these notes where relevant:

- Adult supervision recommended.
- Do not eat experiment seeds.
- Wash hands after handling seeds, soil, compost, or plant materials.
- Do not touch unknown outdoor plants.
- Use adult help for cutting fruit, stems, or plant samples.
- Avoid mold exposure. Do not touch mold directly.
- Check allergies before using food or plant materials.
- Keep small seeds and materials away from children who may put them in their mouths.

## Activity-Specific Safety

### Seed Activities

Do not eat experiment seeds. Keep damp materials clean and discard if mold appears.

### Soil Activities

Wash hands after touching soil. Avoid inhaling dry soil dust.

### Compost Activities

Use adult supervision. Do not touch mold or decomposing material directly. Avoid meat, dairy, oily foods, or unsafe scraps.

### Cutting Activities

Adult cutting required for fruit, stems, celery, flowers, or plant samples.

## Rule

No classroom activity is complete without safety notes.
