## Purpose

This file defines how Happy Seed Stories / Seed Valley can grow beyond one book.

## Core Product Ladder

1. Free activity page
2. Single storybook
3. Storybook plus activity PDF
4. Season 1 storybook bundle
5. Teacher guide
6. Classroom experiment pack
7. Observation journal
8. Poster set
9. Vocabulary cards
10. Garden starter kit
11. Read-aloud video scripts
12. Animated shorts
13. Digital learning app or website hub

## Per-Book Companion Assets

Each book can create:

- coloring page
- vocabulary cards
- experiment worksheet
- observation journal page
- parent/teacher guide
- classroom poster
- read-aloud script
- social media learning post
- sticker/character printable

## Season 1 Bundle

Working bundle title: Seed Valley: From Seed to New Seed.

Includes:

- Books 1-8
- plant life-cycle poster
- experiment pack
- vocabulary cards
- observation journal
- teacher guide

## Brand Rule

The product ecosystem must grow from the story and science. Do not create random merchandise that does not support the educational mission.
